About

Interactive web application that allows users to scout basketball games and view advanced statistics. Users are able to input stats from games, or use our interactive application to log stats real time during a game. All player and team statistics are stored in a database so any user can look them up our website.

Mission

Our Mission is to provide athletes, coaches and teams with the platform to gain the right partnership. Using our product athletes are able to show their stats and increase their visiblity to recruiters. Coaches are also able to conduct effective practices, understand their weakness and strengths, motivate their athletes, and instruct athletes in game strategies and techniques where they need it most.
